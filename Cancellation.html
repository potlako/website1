Client Cancellation

  • Layby cancellation submitted within the 1st 14 days of agreement will receive a full refund on all payments made.
  • Layby cancellation submitted after the 1st 14 days will be charged a 35% penalty fee (thus meaning only 65% of payments will be refunded).

Default cancellation

  • Should you not complete your layby within the agreed period your order will automatically be cancelled & only 50% of total installments paid will be refunded (unless an extension/ freeze period had been arranged)
  • A full refund will be processed should I be forced to cancel your layby due to natural disasters / supplier stock issues and/ other circumstances that affect your layby agreement negatively.
